SLAC Thursdays

Do you like programming? Do you want to learn to program? Do you sit and pretend to look busy on your computer, but really you're just intensely focused on Words with Friends? (Still counts.)

SLAC is your weekly excuse to dedicate some serious time to your hot Web 2.0 mashup, learn how to put your simple Python skills to use on the web, or just hang out on Turntable.fm. You don't need to have a project to work on, just drop by! There'll be nachos, music, and couches—no agenda, but lots of extension cords, whiteboards, and emacs hand contortions.

We'll be coding from 8pm till midnight. Come join us for some coding fun.
